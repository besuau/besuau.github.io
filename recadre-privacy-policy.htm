RECADRE PRIVACY POLICY

Using Recadre is 100% private. There is no log in or sign up, no internet connection is required. The program just runs its framing functions on the opened images, and only saves files at the user's request. Recadre does absolutely nothing the user is not aware of.

Specifically:
  • Recadre DOES NOT read, modify, collect or send any data from the end-user computer.

  • Recadre DOES NOT analyze user behavior, nor send usage statistics anywhere.
